AI Survey Dashboard

Static analytics for your team responses.

This dashboard parses your survey workbook, turns free-text responses into theme-level signals, and highlights both division-specific patterns and respondent similarities across the whole dataset.

Division tabs

Switch between the full company view and each worksheet tab to see the right division context.

Shared patterns

High-level themes detected across time sinks, repeated work, and AI opportunity areas.

Consensus and caution

Where the team strongly agrees and where responses introduce guardrails or skepticism.

Division analytics

Each division card summarizes headcount, repeated-task prevalence, strongest themes, and attitude toward AI assistance.

Similarity among individuals

Pairs are scored from shared language across all long-form responses, not just one question.

Notable differences

These signals surface contrast in optimism, repeatability, and concern areas regardless of division.

Respondent explorer

Every respondent is shown with their full answers so you can inspect the raw survey text directly inside the dashboard.
